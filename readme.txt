Field: Check List
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A simple field that represents multiple check boxes.

Version: 1.0.1
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 3 July 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2.0.3


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the 'checklistfield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
   'extensions' folder.

2. Enable it by selecting the "Field: Check List", choose Enable from the
   with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3. You can now add the "Check List" field to your sections.
